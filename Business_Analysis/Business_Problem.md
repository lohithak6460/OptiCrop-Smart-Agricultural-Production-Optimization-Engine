# Business Problem Statement

Traditional agriculture has long suffered from a lack of precision. Farmers frequently grow crops based on historical local trends rather than actual soil compatibility. This has led to two main challenges:

1.  **Soil Depletion & Degradation:** Over-application of fertilizers (such as excess Nitrogen) leading to soil acidification, chemical runoff, and groundwater contamination.
2.  **Crop Failure & Loss:** Planting seeds in sub-optimal climates or wrong soil chemistry (e.g. acidic soil for lime-hating crops), leading to poor yields and financial instability.

**OptiCrop** acts as a decision support system to bridge this gap. By profiling soil variables, the tool advises precisely how to balance inputs, optimizing yields and protecting ecosystems.
